Buttery Spaghetti

Description

Pasta is one of the simplest yet tastiest dishes that anyone can make! For this recipe, you may choose any shape of pasta you please, but we decided to choose spaghetti. If you are vegan, you may enjoy this dish as well, as instead of butter, you can use an equal portion of olive oil, and it will taste just as good!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. spaghetti
  • 15g salt
  • 1 cup parmasian cheese
  • 3 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Add a half gallon water to a medium-sized pot, and bring to rolling boil
  2. Add 15g salt to the boiling water
  3. Add 1 lb. spaghetti to the boiling water
  4. Boil spaghetti for 8-12 minutes, stir periodically
  5. Drain water completely
  6. Stir 3 tbsp butter into the spaghetti
  7. Sprinkle parmasian cheese on top of the spaghetti
