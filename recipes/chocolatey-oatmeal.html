Chocolatey Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a very healthy food, but unfortunately, it does not come delicious on its own. That's why we have curated an amazingly tasty oatmeal dish that will blow your mind!

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats (2 cup)
  • Milk (2 cup)
  • Raisins (10)
  • Peanut butter (2 tbsp)
  • Cinnamon (2 tbsp)
  • Chocolate chips (1/4 cup)
  • Granola (1/4 cup)
  • Maple syrup (3 tsp)
  • Salt (1/4 tsp)
  • Chocolate whey protein (2 scoop)

Steps

  1. Add rolled oats, raisins, salt, and drizzle maple syrup to a medium bowl
  2. Microwave on high for 4 minutes
  3. Let sit for 1 minute
  4. Add peanut butter, chocolate chips, granola, and sprinkle cinnamon
  5. In a separate cup, stir chocolate whey protein in 1 cup water
  6. Stir mixture into the bowl of oats and enjoy
