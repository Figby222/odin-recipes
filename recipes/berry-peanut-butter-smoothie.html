Berry Peanut Butter Smoothie

Description

Fruit can be a very important part of a balanced diet, and what better way to get those antioxidants than in a blended smoothie? That's why here in this recipe, we offer you the most delicious smoothie we have ever blended!

Ingredients

  • 4 frozen strawberries
  • 8 frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 5 frozen leaves of spinach
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Add frozen strawberries, blueberries, banana, and spinach to a blender
  2. Sprinkle 2 tbsp sugar over the frozen fruits and veggie
  3. Add 4 tbsp peanut butter to the blender
  4. Blend ingredients together to get desired consistency
  5. Top with 3 blueberries
