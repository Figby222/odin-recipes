Berry Peanut Butter Smoothie

Description

Fruit can be a very important part of a balanced diet, and what better way to get those antioxidants than in a blended smoothie? That's why here in this recipe, we offer you the most delicious smoothie we have ever blended!

Ingredients

  • Strawberries (4 frozen)
  • Blueberries (8 frozen)
  • raspberries (4 frozen)
  • Banana (1/2 frozen)
  • Spinach (4 leaves)
  • Sugar (2 tbsp)
  • Peanut butter (4 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Add frozen strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, banana, and spinach to a blender
  2. Sprinkle sugar over the frozen fruits and veggie
  3. Add peanut butter to the blender
  4. Blend ingredients together to get desired consistency
  5. (Optional) Top with 3 blueberries
